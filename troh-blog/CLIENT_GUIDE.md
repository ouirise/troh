# How to Add a Blog Post (For Elaine)

## Simple Steps

### 1. Go to Admin Panel
Open your web browser and go to:
```
https://your-website.com/admin
```

### 2. Fill Out the Form

**Title:** What the post is about (example: "My First Day of Chemo")

**Date:** When you want it published (usually today's date)

**Author:** Your name (already filled in as "Elaine Taylor")

**Featured Image:** 
- Drag a photo from your computer and drop it in the box
- OR click the box and select a photo
- Wait for it to upload (you'll see a preview)

**Excerpt:** 
Write 1-2 sentences describing the post. This shows on the blog listing page.

Example: "Sharing my experience from my first chemotherapy treatment and what I learned."

**Content:**
Write your full blog post here.

**Formatting tips:**
- Use `**text**` for **bold text**
- Use `## Heading` for section titles
- Use blank lines between paragraphs
- Paste images here too if you want

### 3. Check the Preview
Look at the "Live Preview" on the right side. Make sure it looks good!

### 4. Save
Click the **"Save Post"** button.

### 5. Done! üéâ
Your post is now live on the website!

## Tips

### Photos
- Use JPG or PNG files
- Keep photos under 5MB (most phone photos are fine)
- Landscape orientation works best

### Writing
- Be yourself - authentic stories connect best
- Share the real experience - good and bad
- Include details that might help others

### Editing Later
If you need to change a post:
1. Look at "Existing Posts" on the right
2. Click the eye icon to view it
3. You can see the JSON data to give to your developer for edits

---

## Need Help?

**If something doesn't work:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page and try again
3. Make sure all required fields (*) are filled
4. Contact your developer with any error messages
